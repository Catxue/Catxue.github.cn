什么是Aurora

Aurora是一个可以在word,Excel,powerpoint,visio中使用LaTeX的一个插件。它可以使你的公式排版更加美观，打印更漂亮和与上下文格式更和谐，那我就给你讲讲三点关于这个插件的好处。

  1. No more mouse-hunting for symbols—enter great-looking math quickly and efficiently using the standard language of scientific typesetting.

  2. Express any scientific concept from mathematics, computer science, chemistry, engineering, and many other areas.

  3. Full integration with Word, PowerPoint, Visio, and Excel.
